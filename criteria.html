The Business

What we look for in a company.

Revenue $20M to $100M+
EBITDA $4M to $15M (sweet spot $5M–$10M)
Transaction Type Control recapitalizations, founder transitions, family-owned businesses, corporate carve-outs, businesses at inflection points
Capital Structure Family office — no fund cycle, no LP timeline, no forced exit horizon
Geography Flexible — Nationwide
Sectors Industry Agnostic — Consumer, energy, housing, healthcare, business services
The Situation

What kind of moment we step into.

The businesses we back are not broken. They are at a threshold — where the next five years could look very different from the last five, and where the difference is partly about capital and mostly about leadership, strategy, and execution.

We look for founder-owned businesses where the original architect is ready for the next chapter. Family businesses where the next generation needs help professionalizing without losing what made the company worth building. Companies at inflection points — entering new markets, absorbing acquisitions, growing into complexity, or simply ready to become more than they have been.

What We Avoid

We are not the right fit for everyone.

  • Businesses where financial engineering is the primary value creation strategy.
  • Situations where culture, leadership, or customer relationships are fundamentally broken.
  • Commodity businesses with no clear path to differentiation.
  • Situations that need a financial sponsor. We are a partner, not a sponsor.
