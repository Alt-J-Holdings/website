Our Partners

Alt+J brings capital and a belief that our communities matter.

We are founders and operators with a deep understanding of the issues faced by our companies. We can relate to our portfolio and the management teams we invest in because we have sat in their seats. We spent meaningful parts of our careers in industries where the connection between business and community was impossible to ignore because the way you ran the business showed up the next morning in a person’s life.

Every Alt+J partner has built businesses, led teams, made payroll, and lain awake at 3am wondering whether the next move was the right one. We bring that operating experience directly into our portfolio engagements as partners present in the rooms where the moments actually happen.

Jamey Edwards

Co-Founder, President &
Operating Partner

Jamey Edwards is a serial entrepreneur, investor, and operator who has spent over two decades building companies in healthcare — and a career defined by the belief that the best investors are the ones who have built something themselves.

As Co-Founder, President & Operating Partner of Alt+J Holdings, Jamey brings a founder's perspective to every portfolio engagement. Before Alt+J, he co-founded and served as CEO of Cloudbreak Health, a telemedicine platform that brought health equity solutions to over 3,000 healthcare venues nationwide, providing interpretation in 250+ languages for patients who otherwise had no voice in their own care. Earlier, as CEO of Emergent Medical Associates, he transformed a small family practice into one of Southern California's leading ER, hospitalist, and anesthesia groups, caring for more than one million patients annually.

Jamey began his career with a decade in investment banking at Lehman Brothers and private equity at Caltius, participating in transactions totaling over $10 billion. That experience taught him how capital works — and his years as a founder taught him how operators actually need it to work. The gap between the two is where Alt+J lives.

He is an active angel investor, advisor, and mentor to entrepreneurs across the country, and serves as an Entrepreneur in Residence at StartUp Health and on the Entrepreneurship@Cornell Advisory Council. Jamey graduated as a Tradition Fellow from Cornell University, earning a BS in Organizational Development and an MBA in Entrepreneurship and Private Equity.

"I've sat in the founder's chair. I know what 3 AM looks like when payroll is a question. Building a business is made up of these moments. We architected Alt+J to be the supportive framework that turns these moments into milestones. It's why companies choose to have us in their corner."
