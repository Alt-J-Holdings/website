Our Partners

Operators first. Investors second.

Alt+J is led by founders and operators who spent their careers building companies in healthcare, technology, and financial services. We've run P&Ls, hired and managed thousands of employees, navigated regulatory environments, and scaled businesses from startup to national platform. We invest in the industries we know because we've operated in them.

That experience shapes how we show up as investors. We don't just write checks - we sit on boards, work alongside management teams, and bring direct operating experience to the decisions that matter most. Our portfolio companies get partners who understand their challenges because we've faced the same ones.

Our Track Record

We've built the kinds of companies
we invest in.

Our partners co-founded Standard Communities, now one of the nation's leading affordable housing platforms — 30,000+ apartments acquired and rehabilitated across 22 states, $3.6 billion in transactions, approximately 60,000 residents served. They co-founded Cloudbreak Health, a telemedicine platform operating in over 3,000 healthcare venues with language access in 250+ languages. They grew Emergent Medical Associates from a small family practice into one of Southern California's leading emergency medicine groups, caring for more than one million patients per year.

Before building companies, our team spent years in investment banking at Lehman Brothers and private equity at Caltius, participating in over $10 billion in transactions. That background taught us how capital works. Our years as founders taught us how operators actually need it to work. Combined: 65+ years operating, 30+ companies founded or backed, and platforms that changed how communities access housing and care. The gap between knowing how capital works and knowing how operators need it to work is where Alt+J lives.
